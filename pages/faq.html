FAQ

What is Paint Visualizer?

Paint Visualizer is an interactive tool designed to help you explore different paint colors and see how they look in your space before you make any commitments.

How does the tool work?

Simply select a room type from our navigation, choose a paint color from the palette, and watch as the overlay simulates the color on the room image.

What rooms can I visualize?

Currently, you can preview colors in living rooms, kitchens, bedrooms, and exterior spaces. We are continually expanding our options.

Do I need to download any software?

No, our tool is completely web-based and optimized for all devices— whether you’re using a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

Can I save or share my designs?

Yes, you can take screenshots of your design or use our share feature to send your ideas to friends, family, or your interior designer.

Who can I contact for support?

For any additional questions or technical support, please visit our Contact page and submit a request.
